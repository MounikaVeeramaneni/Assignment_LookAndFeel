Welcome To Our Food-Nutrition

Healthy living used to seem easy. Not long ago we ate our food from the garden, drank our water from the hose, and played at the neighbor’s house until the sun went down and our moms yelled, “Dinner!!!” Somewhere along the line, though, living healthy became more complicated. More food came from packages, produce became depleted of essential nutrients, (while other things were added), the workforce quadrupled, stress increased, and we traded small and simple for “bigger and better.” But did we? Despite those things we can’t change, and a clock we can’t reverse, we’re still on a mission to take back healthy! You may be one among them who desires a healthier lifestyle but has no clue of where to start. After all, new tips and advice on diet and healthy living emerge daily. You’re tired and confused of sorting through it all, and I get it. That’s why I’m here to help!
